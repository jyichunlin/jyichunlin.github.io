About Me

I am a biomedical visual communicator who currently live and work in Toronto. I completed my bachelor’s degree in life science at National Taiwan University, and I graduated from the University of Toronto with a Masters degree in Biomedical Communications. My interests include visual storytelling with animated films as well as creating interactive web applications to enhance medical learning and patient education.

I am always looking for opportunities to collaborate in medical and scientific projects. Please send me a message if you are interested in working with me or knowing more about what I do.

My Experiences

As a biomedical multimedia communicator at Toronto General Hospital, I develop online modules for continuing medical education that have been used and recognized by healthcare professionals across the world.

I have been collaborated with scientists and educators on creating visual materials and educational tools for the researchers and the general public since completing my bachelor’s degree in Life Science in 2008.
