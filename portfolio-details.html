Human Pancreas and Duodenum

Text book Illustration

Grant’s museum at University of Toronto houses many original dissections that were illustrated in Grant’s Atlas of Anatomy.

This illustration is based on a dissected specimen of digested tract mounted in a glass container in the museum. It focuses on the complex anatomical structures of pancreas, duodenum, jejunum as well as the surrounding vessels. I experimented with the carbon dust technique, trying to depict various tissue textures in order to clearly show the different structures.
